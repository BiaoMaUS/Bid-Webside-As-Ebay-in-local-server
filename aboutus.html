The History behind iPort

Our company got started because of the same reason one would fear an online auction in the first place. The CEO, Mike Yeung, had placed a winning bid in an online auction for a new mouse for his computer back in 2011. His bid was $100! However, when he received the package, he opened it to discover that he had been robbed of his money, and there was no mouse inside the box! He knew there and then that something had to be done about the online auction system. He asked a few of his friends to help him kick off the company, and now we are steadily approaching a well-known status as a safe alternative to online auctioning.

Had a problem with our service?

At iPort, we take pride in knowing that our customers get the products that they pay for, no matter what it may be. That being said, if you receive a package that isn't exactly what you ordered, let us know. We'll get in contact with everyone involved.
